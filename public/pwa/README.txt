PWA (Progressive Web App) Assets

Instructions:
1) Place all files in your project's /public/pwa/ directory.
2) In your app's <Head> (app/layout.tsx) or pages/_document.tsx, link the manifest & favicon:
   <link rel="manifest" href="/pwa/manifest.json" />
   <link rel="icon" href="/pwa/favicon.ico" />

3) Run the project and open it in Chrome.
4) Open DevTools → Application → Manifest to verify icons.
5) On mobile, visit your site and choose “Add to Home Screen.”

Brand Colors:
- Vibrant Yellow: #FFC300 (splash background)
- Deep Black: #000000
- Soft White: #FFFFFF (theme background)
- Neutral Gray: #F5F5F5
